<p>North Country Public Radio talked with Edith Lopardo and Billy Jones about their recollections of Gabe Lopardo and his barber shop, Lopardo's Barber Shop as part of the North Country at Work series. The shop was in business for 65 years, opened by Gabe's father in 1951 until Gabe's retirment in 2020. Read the full interview or listen to the audio clip at the link below!</p>
